Post: New Pan Card Registration , Change / Correction in Pan Card

All Those Candidates who want to register for new Pan Card or Correct their pan card Details can Register here for new Pan card and Correct their Pan Card Details.

Some Important Required Documents

  • 1.Voter Card

  • 2.Adhar Card

  • 3.Driving Licence

  • 4.Domicile Certificate issued by the Government

  • 5.Bank Account Statement

  • 6.Elector's Photo Identity Card

  • 7.Passport

Application Fee

  • Card dispatch within India : Rs. 107

  • dispatch outside India(Foreign Address) : Rs. 989

INSTRUCTION

    : How to Apply For New PAN Card :

  • 1.Click on " New PAN Card Registration" Tab to fill online Application form for New PAN card.

  • 2.Now Fill your PAN Card application form online

  • 3.Select your Required Documents which you have

  • 4.Click on Submit

  • 5.Verify your details and click on Proceed to Payment Button

  • 6.Make payment. This is one time fee. No Hidden or extra cost.

  • 7.After successful Payment, download and print your pre-filled application form .

  • 8.Paste photo, sign, attach documents and post to address (You will get the postal address in instruction sheet.)

  • 9.Now you will get your pan card to your address within 45 Days .
